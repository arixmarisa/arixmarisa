### About Me ˚⟡˖ ࣪

Growing up, I loved playing video games. Eventually, I became just as interested in how they were built as I was in playing them. That curiosity led me to programming and pushed me toward software engineering, where I enjoy creating practical solutions and learning how systems work behind the scenes.

I’m a **Software Engineering student at Shippensburg University of Pennsylvania** and an **Associate Software Engineer at Cinteot Inc.** I also work on campus as a **Student Ambassador** and **Security Operations Center Student Worker**, giving me experience across communication, technical support, cybersecurity, and real-world problem-solving.

I’m pursuing a **B.S. in Software Engineering** with minors in **Computer Science**, **Data Science**, and **Mathematics**. My program is **ABET-accredited**, and I’m expected to graduate in **May 2028**.

My interests include **software development**, **cybersecurity**, **IT**, and **data science**, with a focus on building reliable applications and continuing to strengthen my technical foundation.

- Working with **C#**, **.NET**, troubleshooting, testing, and software documentation  
- Preparing for **CompTIA Security+** through employer-supported certification training  
- Supporting **SOC operations**, vulnerability monitoring, and security-focused workflows
